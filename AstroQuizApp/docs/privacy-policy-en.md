# Privacy Policy - AstroQuiz

**Last updated:** February 7, 2026

AstroQuiz ("we", "our", or "app") is an astronomy quiz application developed by Roberto Muricy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

By using AstroQuiz, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you create an account or log in, we may collect:
- **Email**: For account identification and access recovery
- **Name**: For experience personalization (optional)
- **Profile photo**: If you log in with Google (provided by Google)

### 1.2 Game Progress Data
We automatically collect data related to your use of the app:
- Score and accumulated XP
- Completed and unlocked phases
- Performance statistics (correct answers, errors, streaks)
- Unlocked achievements
- Preference settings (language, sound, vibration)

### 1.3 Technical Data
To ensure the app functions properly:
- Device identifier (for game sessions)
- Device type and operating system
- Error logs (in case of failures)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Save and sync your progress across devices
- Personalize your app experience
- Improve the app and fix issues
- Send game-related notifications (if authorized)

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.**

---

## 3. Data Storage

### 3.1 Local Storage
Your progress data is stored locally on your device using the operating system's secure storage.

### 3.2 Cloud Storage
If you create an account, your data is synced with our servers to allow access on multiple devices. We use:
- **Firebase Authentication** (Google): For secure authentication
- **Secure servers**: For progress storage
